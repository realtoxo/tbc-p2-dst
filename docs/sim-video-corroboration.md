# Sim vs Video Corroboration

This document compares the Phase 1/Phase 2 DST sim outputs against the class-specialist video/transcript layer.

## Verdict

The videos broadly corroborate the priority bands, but not every exact trinket-pair detail. Rogue, Survival Hunter, Fury Warrior, Retribution Paladin, Enhancement Shaman, and Feral Cat have the clearest alignment. Arms Warrior is weaker because the video support is less specific, and the final Warrior ranking depends on the no-Badge/no-Slayer assumption.

## Main Gaps

| Issue | Video layer | Sim layer | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| Warrior trinket assumptions | Warrior videos often discuss Badge of the Swarmguard and older trinket pairings. | Final report assumes Warriors do not have Badge or Slayer's Crest. | Assumption-bound |
| Raid utility | Videos discuss class value and raid usefulness. | Sims measure personal DPS only. | Not tested |
| Survival Hunter | Videos support Hunter DST claims but also emphasize execution. | Survival gets a strong personal DST delta. | Corroborated with execution caveat |
| Feral Cat | Videos and general specialist guidance support Feral taking strong alternatives. | Feral gains only +5.62 DPS from DST over Tsunami in P2. | Corroborated |

## LC Interpretation

- The video layer supports the same broad priority pool as the sim: Rogue, Survival, Fury, Ret, and Enhancement.
- The sim gives Feral a clear Tsunami-track argument.
- The sim supports Arms as eligible, but not as a high-confidence early DST award.
- The sim does not measure raid DPS from Expose Weakness, Blood Frenzy, Ret utility, Enhancement utility, or Feral utility. Use those as separate LC considerations.
